Our story

In memory of the defeat of the King Pig's Castle, the angryneers reproduce this memorial event each year and compete in order to test who has the best slingshot! The Mari Angryneers (portmanteau of Angry Birds and engineers) are divided into five subteams to make this happen:

  • The robot/slingshot team composed of amazing robot/slingshot makers.
  • The video/recruitment team for producing videos that attract new recruits.
  • The website team for familiarizing new recruits with the competition. Come see their amazing work here!
  • The kiosk/design team in charge of rebuilding the King Pig's Castle.
  • The programming team in charge of keeping hackers away.
